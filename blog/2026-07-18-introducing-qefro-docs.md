**Qefro Docs** at [docs.qefro.com](https://docs.qefro.com) is the canonical technical documentation for the Qefro AI Workspace Platform.

<!-- truncate -->

## Why we shipped a dedicated docs site

Product marketing on [qefro.com](https://qefro.com) explains *what* Qefro is. Operators and engineers need *how*: workspace isolation, hybrid retrieval, Business Tools, RBAC, widget embedding, WhatsApp webhooks, and security boundaries.

We modeled the site after documentation teams trust — clear definitions first, then architecture, workflows, APIs, and honest comparisons.

## How to read the docs

1. Start with a **definition** page under Concepts if you are evaluating the product.
2. Follow a **guide** when you are implementing a channel or integration.
3. Use **API** and **Security** when you are integrating or reviewing risk.
4. Use search (`Ctrl` / `⌘` + `K`) for exact terms like `identify()` or webhook paths.
