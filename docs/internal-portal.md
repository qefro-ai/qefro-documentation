## What it is
Internal Portal is the employee-facing AI surface for private operational workflows.

## Why it matters
- Faster internal support
- Controlled access to company knowledge
- Consistent employee experience

## Example use cases
- HR policies
- leave request guidance
- IT support knowledge
- expense and approval information

## Permissions
Use organization roles, teams, and workspace access to scope employee data exposure.
