## How does authentication work?
Your backend decides authentication; Qefro orchestrates.

## Who sends OTP?
Your backend provider.

## Can I mix REST and SDK tools?
Yes.

## Does Qefro store customer passwords?
No.

## How do I deploy quickly?
Use docs/deployment, docs/docker, and docs/kubernetes.

## How do I debug failed tool calls?
Check SDK connection status, test endpoint, sync output, and tool logs.
